About the Tournament

Three Days. Two Courses.
One Champion.

Every summer, the crew makes the trip to Penn National Golf Club in Fayetteville, Pennsylvania — surrounded by cornfields, tucked into the rolling hills of Franklin County. What started with 8 guys in 2019 has grown into a 20-man, three-day tournament that everyone pencils in before anything else.

The format is simple but unforgiving: four team rounds across Iron Forge and Founders, alternating between Better Ball and 2-Man Scramble, with teams reshuffled every round. Then Sunday morning: individual stroke play. No partner. No excuses.
